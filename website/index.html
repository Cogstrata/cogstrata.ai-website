Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Cogstrata

How is Cogstrata different from other data providers?

Most providers sell static annual snapshots. Cogstrata uses AI to continuously re-process signals monthly, reflecting current market reality. We also include 60+ exclusive attributes (OAC classification, deprivation scores, food desert flags) unavailable anywhere else.

What data attributes are included?

Six categories: Location & Classification, Wealth & Deprivation, Housing & Property, Education & Skills, Household Composition, and Mobility & Retail Access. Full data dictionary available on request.

How do I access the data?

Four options: real-time REST API, batch file export, cloud marketplace listings (AWS Data Exchange, Snowflake), or direct CRM/CDP integration. Our team recommends the best fit for your stack.

Is the data GDPR compliant?

Yes. All data is aggregated at location/output area level — no individual PII. We hold no personal data. Fully compliant with UK GDPR, Data Protection Act 2018, and US privacy frameworks.

How do I get started?

Register your interest and we'll set up a free sample run against your own data within 24 hours. No contract needed to evaluate. You'll see results immediately.
