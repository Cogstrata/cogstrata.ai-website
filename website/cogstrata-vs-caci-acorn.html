Cogstrata vs CACI Acorn

Three reasons teams switch from Acorn to Cogstrata

🕐

Data that reflects today, not 2011

CACI Acorn's core geodemographic taxonomy is anchored to the 2011 census. Cogstrata uses continuous AI inference across housing, macroeconomic, and behavioural signals to keep classifications accurate in real time.

⚡

Built for AI and API-first workflows

Acorn was designed for batch exports and BI dashboards. Cogstrata is structured for direct agent ingestion — self-documenting labels, machine-readable schemas, and a REST API that returns enriched records in milliseconds.

🔒

Postcode-level precision, zero PII

Cogstrata operates at the postcode sector level, meaning there's no personal data at any point in the pipeline. GDPR compliance is by design, not by policy.
