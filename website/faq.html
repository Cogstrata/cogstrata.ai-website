Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Cogstrata different from existing data providers?

Most providers sell static data compiled from annual surveys. Cogstrata uses AI to continuously re-process and update its data, so every attribute reflects current reality — not last year's picture. We also include unique derived metrics, such as retail accessibility scores, banking desert flags, and commute-mode splits, that aren't available anywhere else.

What data attributes are included?

Every Cogstrata record covers six attribute categories: Location & Classification (OAC Supergroup, ward, rural/urban); Wealth & Deprivation (average income, IMD decile, claimant count, insolvency rate); Housing & Property (tenure split, Land Registry prices, EPC energy rating); Education & Skills (qualification levels); Household Composition (single-person, families, lone parents); and Mobility & Retail Access (commute mode, nearest retailer, food desert flag, banking access). Full data dictionary available on request.

How do I access the data?

Four options: real-time REST API (per-lookup or bulk), scheduled batch file export, native listings on AWS Data Exchange, Snowflake Marketplace, and Google Cloud Marketplace, or direct CRM/CDP integration. Your solution engineer will recommend the right fit for your stack.

Is the data GDPR and privacy compliant?

Yes. All Cogstrata data is aggregated at postcode or output area level — no individual-level personal data is stored or transmitted. We hold no PII. Our data processing is fully compliant with UK GDPR, the Data Protection Act 2018, and US privacy frameworks.

How do I get started?

Register your interest and a member of the team will set up a free sample run against your own postcodes within 24 hours. No contract needed to evaluate the data.
