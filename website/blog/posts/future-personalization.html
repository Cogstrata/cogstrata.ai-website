Why AI-refreshed data outperforms the annual survey

Published on March 16, 2026

Most demographic data products are built on surveys and census snapshots — accurate at the moment of collection, and increasingly stale from that point forward. By the time a dataset has been compiled, validated, licensed, and integrated into your stack, the world it describes may be 18 months old.

Cogstrata takes a different approach. Rather than periodic data collection, our AI continuously reprocesses signals from land registry transactions, EPC certificates, retail openings, insolvency filings, claimant records, and other real-world event streams. The result is a postcode-level picture that updates as the world updates — not on an annual cycle.

Still waiting for last year's survey data?

AI-refreshed attributes update continuously, not annually. See the difference on your own customer postcodes.

The practical difference is significant. A postcode that was middle-income in a 2022 survey may have experienced a substantial shift in household composition, financial stress, or retail access since then. Acting on the old picture means misallocating budget, missing the right customers, or mispricing risk. Always-on AI processing closes that gap.
