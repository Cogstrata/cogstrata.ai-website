60 things a postcode can tell you about your customer

Published on March 16, 2026

A postcode is not just an address. In the UK and US, the area covered by a single postcode holds a remarkably consistent set of socioeconomic, lifestyle, and behavioural signals — signals that, when properly structured, can transform how a business understands its customers.

Cogstrata's postcode data covers six categories of insight: the socioeconomic profile of residents (income, deprivation, qualifications); the physical character of the neighbourhood (housing tenure, property values, energy ratings); the composition of households (families, singles, lone parents); the lifestyle classification of the community (OAC segment); and two categories of access — retail and financial — that reveal how connected or isolated a community is from the services most people take for granted.

That last category — access data — is where Cogstrata is genuinely unique.
