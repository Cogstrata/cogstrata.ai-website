Three reasons teams switch from Mosaic to Cogstrata

📅

Three years is too long

Mosaic's last major refresh took three years. In the intervening period, entire neighbourhoods changed — through cost-of-living pressures, hybrid working patterns, and demographic shifts. Cogstrata updates continuously, so your data reflects reality, not a historical snapshot.

📊

5,000 attributes vs 450

Mosaic's 450 variables cover the essentials. Cogstrata's 5,000+ derived attributes go deeper — covering mortgage pressure deltas, discretionary spend estimates, retail access scores, and AI-inferred life-stage signals that Mosaic simply doesn't carry.

💰

Usage-based pricing that scales with you

Mosaic comes with an annual licence and bureau pricing model. Cogstrata charges per enrichment, meaning early-stage teams can access the same quality of intelligence as enterprise clients without a six-figure commitment.
